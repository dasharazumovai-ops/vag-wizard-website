Our Story

Built on experience, growing with purpose

VAG Wizard began its journey in 2016, originally operating under the name C.A Car Wizard. From the beginning, the focus was simple: provide reliable, high-quality repairs using proper diagnostics, professional equipment and careful attention to detail.

Over the years, the workshop built a strong reputation working with Volkswagen Group vehicles, including Volkswagen, Audi, SEAT and Skoda. Through hands-on experience and continuous investment in the right tools and technology, the business developed specialist knowledge in diagnostics, servicing and transmission repair.

As the workshop grew and its focus on VAG vehicles became clearer, the business evolved into VAG Wizard Ltd, marking a new chapter and a stronger commitment to becoming a dedicated VAG specialist.

Today, VAG Wizard continues to operate with the same principles that built its reputation: precision diagnostics, specialist VAG knowledge, professional equipment, and honest, transparent service.

Looking ahead, VAG Wizard will continue building on its reputation for quality and reliability. Future plans include expanding operations with a second workshop in Limassol and introducing a vehicle rental service, allowing us to support more drivers across Cyprus.
